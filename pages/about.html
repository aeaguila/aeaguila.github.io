About Me...

I am a researcher, a needs-finder, and a problem-solver. I feed on discoveries that improve lives and make navigating this complex world simpler. In Fall 2014 I started the Masters Program at SJSU in Human Factors and Ergonomics. This last year I have worked on projects ranging from redesigning part of an app to performing a usability test on a Lexus Multifunction display. I love asking questions and uncovering insights about how people use products. I am excited to further develop my expertise and be involved in every stage of the design process.
